Experience Designer Portfolio

This is not a typical designer’s site. It’s black, not white! Hang on, where’s the parallax scrolling? I don’t see any cute lil icons! Nope, none of it. Don’t get me wrong, all that stuff is lovely, but I don’t have Squarespace. I am designing and building this living document from scratch.


My website is a representation of my self, and as such it evolves as I do. There will likely be some subtle changes the next time you visit, and the time after that. Hardly a day goes by when I don’t think, “Hey! Wouldn’t that be neat?” as I’m walking, or showering, or stuffing my face. (I love food). It is my personal proving ground: I can take ideas here, build them, try them out, see what works, and make them better.


Poke around as much as you like. Feedback is greatly appreciated  ;-)
