A mobile device-based resource to help refugees.

Google Beacons are WiFi-enabling network towers. Each Beacon is stocked with over 1000 Light Phones.

The Beacons tether together wirelessly, creating a wide net of WiFi connectivity for internet access.

Users can then access a specialized suite of Google products, all available through a Google Account.

Each Beacon serves as a solar powered charging station to enable sustained usage for each phone.
