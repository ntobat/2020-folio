A mobile device-based solution for those affected by the Syrian Refugee Crisis.

Introducing Google Refuge.

Over 25 million people globally have been forced to flee their homes from persecution or violence. They are now refugees.

Refugees lose nearly everything as a result of needing to escape quickly. This often includes separation from family, friends, and loved ones.

Google Refuge provides connectivity for those who have lost everything else.

The first phase of this project is focused on the current Syrian Refugee Crisis, an event that is now the largest refugee crisis of our time.

The need for an information service and resource is critical to coordinate with separated loved ones, manage documentation, and get information. Refugees currently spend 1/3 of what they have on communication, trying to find asylum, get help, or locate loved ones.
