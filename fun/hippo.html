Brand Identity

Hippo

Creative Agency

This is a fun take on a basic brand identity for a conceptual creative agency.


My team spent quite a bit of time brainstorming the values we wanted our agency to embody. We wanted to not only represent our agency, but to represent each of us and our personalities as well.


We decided on six words that would guide our work process in all future projects. My role was to develop the brand identity, and I wanted to do it in a way that not only lays out text styles and a color palette, but does so in a way that echoes our core values.
