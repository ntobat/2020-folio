AMERICA 2050

Project Title

AMERICA 2050 is a satirical gaming platform that invites users to learn more about politics and how "the system" works by playing games.

Project Description

CONTEXT

If you’re alive and reading this, you know that American politics are chaotic, convoluted, and often just plain crazy. The situation isn't helped much by Americans' lack of civic knowledge. Getting young people out to vote is difficult and imperative.

CLIENT

Crooked Media is a liberal political media company with a host of podcasts that aims to promote open conversation and support political participation.

CHALLENGE

The biggest challenge was interesting people in a topic that, on the face of it, appears esoteric, complex, and just plain boring. AMERICA 2050 circumvents the usual barriers to entry around understanding the US government by gamifying the learning process.

CONCEPT

AMERICA 2050: a satirical web-based gaming platform featuring 8-bit style games modeled after current events. Games feature educational content and link to relevant podcast episodes.
